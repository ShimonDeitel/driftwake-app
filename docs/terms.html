1. What Driftwake is

Driftwake estimates the moment you fall asleep from on-device motion and microphone signals, then schedules a wake alarm a duration of your choosing after that estimated moment. It is a personal wellness and alarm-clock tool, not a medical device.

2. Not medical advice

Driftwake does not diagnose, treat, or monitor any sleep disorder, and its onset detection is a best-effort heuristic based on stillness and quiet — not clinical-grade sleep staging. If you have concerns about your sleep, insomnia, sleep apnea, or any other condition, talk to a qualified healthcare provider.

3. Alarm reliability

Driftwake's wake alarm depends on iOS scheduling your device's local notification correctly, on your phone having battery and not being powered off, and — for automatic detection — on iOS allowing the app to keep sampling in the background. We work to make this reliable (including using the same background-audio technique other sleep-tracking apps rely on), but we cannot guarantee the operating system will never suspend the app early. For anything you cannot afford to sleep through, keep a backup alarm.

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID, renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the current period ends, and can be managed or cancelled anytime in your device's Settings under your Apple ID.
